Document Purpose & Agile Integration

This Product Requirement Document (PRD) template is specifically designed for digital solutions developed using Agile methodology. Unlike traditional PRDs that attempt to capture entire product requirements upfront, this template focuses on feature-level documentation that supports iterative development and continuous collaboration between product, design, and engineering teams.

Key Agile Principles Integrated

  • Feature-focused scope rather than product-wide documentation
  • Collaborative refinement instead of requirements handover
  • Iterative development support with sprint-ready user stories
  • Continuous feedback mechanisms built into the process
  • Flexible documentation that evolves with the product
